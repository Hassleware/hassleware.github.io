#10

This piece is a reimagining of Dave Blunts as a mastermind character who gains power through what he consumes. I paired that idea with a technological throne to show a sense of ascension and control.

Process & inspiration

  • Experimental character concept
  • Combined humour with a darker sci-fi tone
  • Designed the throne to suggest power and authority
  • If I revisited this piece, I would add more detail to the lower half of the throne
