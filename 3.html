#3

I designed these demons to see if they could fit into the world of DOOM, which has very hostile and aggressive enemy designs. I chose to pose one of them sitting down to suggest intelligence and awareness beyond pure rage or instinct.

Process & inspiration

  • Inspired by DOOM enemy designs and atmosphere
  • Designed original creatures rather than copying existing demons
  • Experimented with body language to imply intelligence
  • Focused on silhouettes and hostile shapes
