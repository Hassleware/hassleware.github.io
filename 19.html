#19

This dragon was my attempt to create something that feels faithful to classic dragon designs while still having unique features. I added a magma-like colour gradient and an armour-style skull to make it feel powerful and distinct.

Process & inspiration

  • Inspired by traditional dragon anatomy
  • Added magma textures to suggest heat and strength
  • Designed the skull to feel armoured and aggressive
  • Focused on balancing familiar and original elements
