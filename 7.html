#7

This is my interpretation of Nurgle from Warhammer 40,000. I imagined him in front of a cauldron, endlessly trying to create the perfect disease, to show his cosmic power and complete indifference to life.

Process & inspiration

  • Inspired by Nurgle as the embodiment of decay and stagnation
  • Focused on scale and presence rather than action
  • Designed the pose to feel calm and patient rather than aggressive
  • Wanted him to feel inevitable, not emotional
