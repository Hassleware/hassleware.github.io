#33

This piece was meant to show a UFO flying over a mountain range at sunset in a stylised way. Looking back, it now feels more like a crash scene because of the fiery colour coming from the craft, which I think changes the mood in an interesting way.

Process & inspiration

  • Stylised rather than realistic approach
  • Experimented with sunset colours
  • Learned how colour can change narrative
  • Early exploration of sci-fi atmosphere
