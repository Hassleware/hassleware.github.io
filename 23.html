#23

This is a mural-style interpretation of Tzeentch from Warhammer 40,000. He stood out to me as a clever, manipulative, and malicious character rather than a purely physical threat.

Process & inspiration

  • Inspired by religious murals and symbolic art
  • Focused on intelligence and manipulation over brute force
  • Used stylised shapes instead of realism
  • Designed it to feel worshipped and feared
