#1

I made this piece to practice lighting while hiding most of the character’s design. I wanted to see if I could still make the monster feel 3D and have depth, even though it’s a 2D drawing. By keeping most of the body in shadow and focusing on the face, I tried to guide where the viewer looks and control the mood.

Process & inspiration

  • Focus: lighting, depth, and atmosphere
  • Experimented with obscuring form using shadow
  • Inspired by a comic book shading tutorial that showed how lighting can completely change the feel of a character
  • Goal: improve how I convey 3D form in 2D art
