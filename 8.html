#8

For this version of Nurgle, I went for a more symbolic, mural-like style. I wanted it to feel like something ancient, carved or worshipped rather than a literal depiction.

Process & inspiration

  • Used dithering to create a darker, engraved texture
  • Aimed for a cave painting or religious mural feel
  • Designed it as if it was created by followers or creatures that worship Nurgle
  • Focused on symbolism over realism
