#9

I drew Arishem from Marvel’s Eternals because his design stood out to me as both mechanical and deeply alien at the same time. I really enjoyed this piece because getting the lighting and shading accurate to the film pushed my skills.

Process & inspiration

  • Inspired by Arishem’s scale and presence in Eternals
  • Focused on accurate lighting and atmosphere
  • Practised controlled shading to match a cinematic reference
  • Explored how to make something feel ancient and advanced at once
