#6

This is my interpretation of the Void Dragon from Warhammer 40,000. I wanted it to feel like a living mechanical city rather than just a creature, with a sense of intelligence and agency behind it. I also tried to make it more traditionally dragon-like compared to the official model.

Process & inspiration

  • Inspired by the Void Dragon and the Necrons from Warhammer 40,000
  • Designed it as an underground mechanical city made of bunker-like structures
  • Used glowing green lights as eyes to imply awareness and consciousness
  • Focused on scale, power, and the idea of something immense being trapped
  • Intentionally avoided blocky, modern military shapes by using sharper, more angular sci-fi forms
  • This piece is unfinished and I plan to push it further with more detail and refinement
