#36

This is a dragon like creature I designed when I was younger. I wanted it to feel imposing without relying on more generic Western dragon traits, so I leaned more toward Eastern dragon design with a longer snake like body and dinosaur inspired features. This piece represents my early interest in creature design and experimenting with different influences.

Process & inspiration

  • Influenced by Eastern dragon designs
  • Emphasised serpentine anatomy
  • Combined dragons with dinosaurs
  • Early exploration of creature design choices
