#36

I enjoyed working on this piece because drawing female anatomy makes me rely less on heavy bulk and more on proportion, balance and posture to make the character convincing. I also checked the figure using the traditional heads tall proportion method to keep the anatomy believable.

The design takes some influence from characters such as Chell from Portal and Lara Croft from Tomb Raider. Their builds feel more agile and controlled, which suited the idea of a character equipped for light war gear where endurance and precision matter more than raw strength. If I revisit the character, I would place her in a less hostile but still practical environment, or redesign her with fuller military armour to reflect battlefield use rather than an expedition setting.

Process & inspiration

  • HB sketch with 6B shading
  • Study of female anatomy and proportion
  • Influenced by game character design (Portal, Tomb Raider)
  • Focus on agility and believable combat gear
  • Futuristic environmental redesign
