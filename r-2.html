#32

This was my final piece for GCSE Art. I painted an astronaut looking up at the sky as rockets launch behind them. I blended the sky from light blue into black to show a cooler sunset and contrast it with the red glow from the rocket flames.

Process & inspiration

  • GCSE coursework final piece
  • Practised colour blending and gradients
  • Used contrast between cool and warm colours
  • Focused on atmosphere and scale
