#5

I created this alien to see if I could design something that felt powerful and threatening while still being believable anatomically. I wanted it to look alien, but still grounded enough to fit into a sci-fi action setting like Invincible.

Process & inspiration

  • Inspired by Invincible and its more monstrous characters
  • Focused on muscular scale and physical power rather than weapons
  • Tried to balance alien anatomy with believable muscle structure
  • If I revisited this piece, I would add more shading and colour
